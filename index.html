COVID-19 Updates
Masks
  • Do not hang your masks around the neck as germs can easily reach your mouth and nose.
  • Do not share masks.
  • Replace the masks with a new one as soon as it is damp or dirty and do not re-use single use masks
  • Cover your mouth and nose with the mask and make sure there are no gaps between your face and the mask.
