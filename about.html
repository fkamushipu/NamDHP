About Us

Nam DHP is a website which stands for Namibia's Digital Health Pass. It is designed in 2021, by a UNAM student with a notion to allow Namibians and International travellers to have their COVID-19 related information stored electronically.



Mission

To enable the verifiability of Covid-19 immunity among Namibians and travellers using QR code
