What Nam DHP is?

Nam DHP is a Namibian Digital Health Pass .
That helps with collecting, storing, tracking as well as managing medical information for Namibians and international travellers.
Eliminating the current paper based health pass.

How Nam DHP works?

If you have an existing account

Follow these steps :

  1. Go to Register page ;
  2. Enter your password and username in login form and press the login button ;
  3. Your information should appear;
  4. To get your information in a QR code: Click on the button labelled Generate QR Code;
  5. Your QR Code will then appear;
  6. To make an appointment enter your reason and send;
  7. Appointment approval will be send to your email including the scheduled day;


If you do not have an existing account

Follow these steps :

  1. Go to Register page :
  2. Click on the register button and fill out your information;
  3. Click the register button to register;
  4. Close the form if you have prompted that you are successfully registerred;
  5. Go to Login:
  6. Enter your password and username in login form and press the login button ;
  7. Your information should appear under vaccination card heading;
  8. To get your information in a QR code: Click on the button labelled Generate QR Code;
  9. Your QR Code will then appear;
  10. To make an appointment enter your reason and send;
  11. Appointment approval will be send to your email including the scheduled day;
Why Nam DHP?

Nam DHP is a better solution to handle the restrictive pysical distancing measures imposed to control the spred of severe acute respiratory syndrome corona virus.
The current system paper based is not convenient as far as physical contact is concerned, followed by numerous disadvantages that involved with the paper based system.
To list a couple of drawbacks:
difficulty to retrieve data once it gets lost and the illegibility of handwritten notes which can contribute to medical errors.
