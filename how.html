What Nam DHP is?

Nam DHP is a Namibian Digital Health Pass .
That helps with collecting, storing, tracking as well as managing medical information for Namibians and international travellers.
Eliminating the current paper based health pass.

Why Nam DHP?

Nam DHP is a better solution to handle the restrictive pysical distancing measures imposed to control the spred of severe acute respiratory syndrome corona virus.
The current system paper based is not convenient as far as physical contact is concerned, followed by numerous disadvantages that involved with the paper based system.
To list a couple of drawbacks:
difficulty to retrieve data once it gets lost and the illegibility of handwritten notes which can contribute to medical errors.

How Nam DHP works?

The user has to enter the information on website.
The user vaccination status as well vaccine information is added by health professionals
The user can then get a QR code by clicking generate QR button from their profile on the website.
